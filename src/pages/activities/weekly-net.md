Winnebago County ARES/RACES meets on the air every Sunday at 9PM local time, on the 147.240 (PL 100.0 Hz) repeater.  The net is open to anybody wishing to checking in.

## Net Control Schedule
There is currently no set net control schedule. Should the normal NCS not be available, any member may step in as net control.
